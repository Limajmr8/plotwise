About Plotwise

Built on real data, for real decisions.

Data Sources

  • Crop Production Data
    District-wise Area, Production & Yield data for 2023-24. 576 records across 16 districts and 44 crops.
  • Market Prices
    MSP 2023-24 (CACP) + Agmarknet APMC data for Nagaland. 33 crops with price anchors.
  • Weather
    Live forecast from Open-Meteo. 7-day forecasts updated every 15 minutes. GPS coordinates for all 16 district HQs.
  • Disease Detection
    AI model trained on PlantVillage dataset (Penn State). 24 disease classes across 9 crop types.

Methodology

All crop area, production, and yield figures are sourced from verified 2023-24 agriculture records for Nagaland. No data has been interpolated, estimated, or generated.

Disease detection uses deep learning with a three-tier confidence system: above 70% (confident), 55-70% (low confidence), below 55% (uncertain).

Market prices are anchored to MSP 2023-24. Actual market prices fluctuate and are indicative only.
