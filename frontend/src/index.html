What Plotwise Does
Four tools.
One platform.
Nagaland is still a food deficit state despite over 60% of its population farming the land. Plotwise puts real-time intelligence directly into farmers' hands — and gives the Agriculture Department a unified view across all 16 districts.

How It Works
Three steps. Zero complexity.
01
Photograph your crop
Open Plotwise, select your crop and district, and snap a photo of the affected leaf. Works on any smartphone camera.
02
AI analyses instantly
Our EfficientNetB0 model processes the image in seconds — identifying the disease, severity, and matching it against Nagaland-specific crop data.
03
Get treatment + next steps
Receive specific treatment recommendations, prevention tips, nearest KVK contact, and links to government schemes — all in one result.
