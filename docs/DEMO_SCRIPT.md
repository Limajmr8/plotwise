# Plotwise — 15-Minute Demo Script

**Audience**: Joint Director, Nagaland Agriculture Department  
**Setup**: Laptop (projected) + Android phone (passed around)  
**Tone**: Confident, practical, government-appropriate. No tech jargon.

---

## BEFORE YOU START (5 min prior)

1. Open laptop browser → `https://limajmr-plotwise.hf.space`
2. Open phone → Plotwise app (verify it loads)
3. Confirm: WiFi working, projector connected, PDF printed
4. Set browser zoom to 100%, clear any browser tabs except Plotwise
5. Phone brightness 100%, Do Not Disturb ON

---

## MINUTE 0–1: Introduction

**[Stand, face audience]**

> "Good morning, Sir. I'm Limawapang Jamir. Today I want to show you Plotwise — a platform we've built specifically for Nagaland agriculture. It uses your department's own data — 576 real records from all 16 districts — combined with AI-powered disease detection and live market intelligence."

**[Gesture to screen]**

> "Everything I'm about to show you is live and working. Not a mockup, not a prototype."

---

## MINUTE 1–3: Landing Page & Overview

**[Scroll slowly through landing page]**

1. Point out the animated statistics: "16 districts, 44 crops, 24 disease classes our AI can detect"
2. Click **Language Toggle** (EN → Nagamese): "It works in Nagamese too — for our farmers in remote areas"
3. Switch back to English
4. Point to the crop price ticker: "Live market prices scrolling here — updated daily"

> "This one platform replaces five different tools an extension officer currently juggles."

---

## MINUTE 3–5: Disease Detection (STAR FEATURE)

**[Click Disease Detection tab]**

> ⚠️ USE A VETTED IMAGE from the `demo-images/` folder (or a single leaf on a
> plain background). Pick the crop the filename tells you, e.g. Potato.

1. Select **Potato** from crop dropdown
2. Select **Kohima** as district
3. Upload a vetted Late Blight image (verified ~99% confident)
4. **Wait for result** (~3-5 seconds)

**[Point to result]**

> "The AI identified Late Blight with high confidence. It immediately tells the farmer: what the disease is, how severe it is, and exactly what treatment to apply."

> "And notice — it gives a confidence level. If it's ever unsure, it says so and tells the farmer to retake the photo or consult an officer, instead of guessing. It will never hand a farmer wrong advice that costs them a crop."

> "Every detection is logged with who reported it, when, and where. Full accountability."

**[Show heatmap updating]**

> "See how the disease map updated? District officers can see outbreak patterns in real time across all 16 districts."

---

## MINUTE 5–7: Market Prices + PDF Report

**[Click Market Prices tab]**

1. Scroll through the 33 crops with prices
2. Point out: "Current market price, MSP comparison, and trend direction"
3. Filter by "Ginger": "Your officer asks about ginger — one click"

**[Click Download PDF]**

> "Now here's what I want to hand you..."

**[Hold up printed PDF]**

> "This is a complete District Agriculture Intelligence Report. Your department's name is on it. It has crop production data, disease surveillance, market prices — all auto-generated. One click."

**[Pass PDF to Joint Director]**

---

## MINUTE 7–9: Planting Calendar + Scheme Finder

**[Click Planting Calendar tab]**

> "Farmers always ask 'when do I sow?' — this shows them."

1. Point to status badges: "See — Maize shows 'harvest time' and Jhum Paddy is 'growing' because it's July. This updates itself every month."
2. Switch district to Dimapur: "Different districts, different crops appear"

**[Click Scheme Finder tab]**

1. Enter: Crop = Ginger, District = Mon
2. Click "Find My Schemes"

> "PM-KISAN appears — it's universal. But look — NE Horticulture Mission also appears because ginger qualifies. Farmers miss these schemes because they don't know they're eligible."

---

## MINUTE 9–11: Weather + Chat (Live Intelligence)

**[Click Weather tab]**

1. Show Kohima weather: temperature, humidity, 7-day forecast
2. Point to farming advisory: "This tells farmers: good day to spray, don't irrigate today — context-aware"

**[Open Chat widget — floating bubble]**

1. Type "What's the price of ginger?" → Price response with MSP
2. Type "My potato has spots" → Disease info with treatment
3. Type in Nagamese: "ginger-r daam" → Responds correctly

> "Farmers can just ask questions in their own language. No training needed."

---

## MINUTE 11–13: Mobile App (PASS THE PHONE)

**[Pick up phone, show screen to audience]**

> "Now — this is what the farmer actually carries in the field."

1. Show home screen: KPI cards, weather, price ticker
2. Open Disease Detection: "They point the camera at a leaf..."
3. Take/show photo of prepared leaf
4. Show result appearing

> "Same AI, same accuracy, works on a ₹8,000 Android phone."

**[Pass phone to Joint Director]**

> "Please try it yourself, Sir. The camera works — point it at this leaf sample."

**[While phone is being passed, continue talking]**

> "Notice the profile at the top — it shows who this extension officer is. Every report is tracked."

---

## MINUTE 13–14: Offline — THE DIFFERENTIATOR

> "Now the most important part — because we know Nagaland's connectivity."

**[Open the app once with internet first so the phone caches everything, THEN turn on Airplane Mode in front of them]**

1. Turn on **Airplane Mode** (show them the phone — no signal, no wifi)
2. Go to **Detect** → pick a crop → upload/photograph a leaf → **Analyse**
3. Result appears — disease, confidence, treatment — **with the network off**

> "This is the AI running ON the phone, not in the cloud. A farmer in a village with zero signal photographs a sick leaf and gets a diagnosis with treatment — offline, on an ordinary Android phone."

4. Show **Market**, **Farm → Calendar/Schemes** still working in airplane mode

> "Prices, planting calendar, scheme finder — all work offline too. And every diagnosis made offline is saved on the phone and automatically syncs to your department's surveillance map the moment they're back in coverage. No data is ever lost."

**[Turn Airplane Mode off]**

> "No web app an agency builds will do this. This is built for how Nagaland actually connects."

> "And because the AI is on the phone, there's no server bottleneck — it works whether one farmer uses it or a hundred thousand do."

---

## MINUTE 14–15: Closing & Ask

**[Face audience directly]**

> "To summarize — Plotwise gives your department:
> 1. **AI disease detection that runs OFFLINE, on the farmer's own phone** — built for Nagaland's connectivity
> 2. Prices, calendar, and schemes that also work with no network
> 3. Offline diagnoses that sync to a live department surveillance map
> 4. AI disease detection across 24 diseases, 9 crops
> 5. Market intelligence for 33 crops with MSP tracking
> 6. Planting calendar + scheme matching for all 16 districts
> 7. Full audit trail, Nagamese support, works on any Android phone"

**[Pause]**

> "This is built on YOUR data — the 2023-24 district-wise production records from this office. It's ready to deploy. With your department's support, we can pilot this with extension officers across three districts within 60 days."

**[Hand over the printed PDF report if not already passed]**

> "Thank you, Sir. I'm happy to answer any questions."

## BACKUP PLANS

| Problem | What to Say / Do |
|---------|-----------------|
| Server is slow | "The AI is processing — let me show you the dashboard while we wait" → switch to prices/calendar |
| No internet | Switch to mobile hotspot; show offline mode |
| Camera fails on phone | Use pre-uploaded vetted images from demo-images/ folder |
| Detection says "Uncertain" | Own it as a feature: "It refuses to guess when unsure — safer for farmers. The pilot fine-tunes it on Nagaland field photos." Then upload a vetted image to show a clean result. |
| PDF won't generate | Open browser tab: `/api/export/report?district=Kohima` |
| Question you don't know | "That's a great point, Sir. I'll prepare that data and share it with your office this week." |

- ❌ "We're still building..." → Everything shown works today
- ❌ "It's just a prototype" → "It's ready for pilot deployment"
- ❌ Technical API/database terms → "The system stores/tracks/generates"
